Use #1: Completed Actions

We use the past simple to talk about completed actions in the past.

Examples
  • I went to Spain last year.
  • They bought a new house last month.
  • We watched the movie last night.

Use #2: Facts or Generalisations

Past simple can also be used to talk about facts or generalisations in the past.

Examples
  • People lived in caves in the past.
  • They did not use mobile phones in the 19th century.

Use #3: Sequence of Events

We can use the past simple to talk about a sequence of events in the past.

Examples
  • He woke up, brushed his teeth and went to work.
  • First, they arrived at the station, then they took the train.
